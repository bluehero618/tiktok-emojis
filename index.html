Frequently Asked Questions

How to use TikTok emojis?

Click the "Copy" button on any emoji card to copy it to your clipboard. Then you can paste it in TikTok comments, bio, or anywhere else that supports emojis.

How to download TikTok emojis as PNG?

Click the "Download" button on any emoji card to download a high-quality PNG image of the emoji.

Can I use TikTok emojis outside of TikTok?

Yes! You can copy any TikTok emoji and use it in other social media platforms, messaging apps, or anywhere that supports Unicode emojis.

How to get TikTok emojis on keyboard?

TikTok emojis are standard Unicode emojis that are already available on most modern keyboards. If you're looking for specific TikTok-themed stickers, those are only available within the TikTok app.

What are trending TikTok emojis?

Trending TikTok emojis are those that are currently popular in TikTok comments and videos. You can find them in our "Trending" category.
