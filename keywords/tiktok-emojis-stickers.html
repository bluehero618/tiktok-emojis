How to Use TikTok Emoji Stickers

1. For TikTok Videos

Download the sticker, then in the TikTok video editor, tap "Stickers" and select "Upload" to add your custom emoji sticker. Resize and position it anywhere in your video.

2. For Instagram Stories

After downloading, create a story and tap the sticker icon. Select "Import Image" to use your TikTok emoji sticker. Adjust size and placement as needed.

3. For Graphic Design

Our PNG stickers with transparent backgrounds work perfectly in Photoshop, Canva, and other design software. Simply import and integrate them into your projects.

4. For Thumbnails

Add eye-catching TikTok emojis to your video thumbnails to increase click-through rates and engagement. The transparent background makes them easy to incorporate.

Tips for Using Emoji Stickers Effectively

  • Use sparingly: Too many stickers can overwhelm your video. Choose 1-3 key emojis that enhance your message.
  • Size appropriately: Make stickers large enough to be visible but not so large they distract from your main content.
  • Match the mood: Select emoji stickers that complement the emotional tone of your video.
  • Create contrast: Ensure your stickers stand out against the background by choosing emojis with colors that contrast with your video.
  • Animate thoughtfully: When adding animation to stickers, keep movements subtle to avoid distracting viewers.
