Frequently Asked Questions about TikTok Emoji Lists

How many emojis are available on TikTok?

TikTok supports the full standard Unicode emoji set, which currently includes over 3,600 unique emojis. This collection continues to grow with each Unicode update, allowing TikTok users to express themselves with the latest emoji additions.

Are there TikTok-exclusive emojis?

While TikTok uses the standard Unicode emoji set available on most platforms, the app sometimes features custom emoji-like stickers that are exclusive to TikTok. These can be found in the sticker section when creating content, but they aren't technically emojis in the Unicode sense.

How can I find a specific emoji on TikTok?

To find a specific emoji on TikTok, you can:
1. Use the emoji keyboard on your device when commenting or creating content
2. Browse through the emoji categories (smileys, people, animals, etc.)
3. Use the search function in your device's emoji keyboard if available
4. Reference our complete TikTok emoji list on this page to find what you're looking for

How often are new emojis added to TikTok?

New emojis are added to TikTok when they're approved by the Unicode Consortium and implemented in the Unicode standard. This typically happens once a year with new Unicode releases. TikTok generally supports these new emojis shortly after they're available on major operating systems.
