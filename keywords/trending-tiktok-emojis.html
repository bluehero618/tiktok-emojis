Trending TikTok Emojis FAQ

How often do TikTok emoji trends change?

TikTok emoji trends can change rapidly, sometimes within a few weeks. Major trends typically last 2-3 months, while some evergreen emojis remain popular year-round. We update our trending list weekly.

How can I use trending emojis to boost my TikTok engagement?

Incorporate trending emojis in your video captions, comments, and on-screen text. Using popular emojis can help your content appear more relevant and potentially increase engagement from viewers familiar with current trends.

Are trending emojis the same worldwide?

While some emoji trends are global, others may be region-specific. TikTok's algorithm shows different trends to different users based on location and content preferences. Our trending list focuses on global and US-based trends.

How do I create special emoji combinations that are trending?

You can create special emoji combinations by simply typing multiple emojis together in your TikTok posts. For example, to create the trending "Fairy Bot" (✨🤖✨), just type the sparkle emoji, followed by the robot emoji, and another sparkle emoji.
